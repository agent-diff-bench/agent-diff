# Philosophy of Science

## Introduction

This document explores the philosophical foundations of scientific inquiry, examining questions about the nature of scientific knowledge, methodology, and the relationship between theory and observation.

## Key Topics

### Empiricism vs Rationalism
- The role of observation in scientific discovery
- The limits of inductive reasoning
- Theoretical frameworks and their influence on observation

### Scientific Method
- Hypothesis formation and testing
- The problem of demarcation
- Falsifiability as a criterion for scientific theories

### Ethics in Scientific Research
- Responsible conduct of research
- The social responsibility of scientists
- Conflicts of interest and bias

## Conclusion

Understanding the philosophy of science helps researchers become more reflective practitioners and contributes to better scientific practice.
